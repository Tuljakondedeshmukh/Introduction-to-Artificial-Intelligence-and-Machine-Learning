Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines or computer systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and recognizing images or patterns. AI is used in many modern technologies such as virtual assistants, chatbots, self-driving cars, and recommendation systems on platforms like streaming services and online shopping websites. The goal of AI is to make machines capable of making decisions and performing tasks intelligently.
Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to learn from data and improve their performance without being explicitly programmed. Instead of following fixed instructions, machine learning algorithms analyze data, identify patterns, and make predictions or decisions. For example, ML is used in email spam detection, voice recognition, medical diagnosis, and recommendation systems. By training models with large amounts of data, machine learning helps computers become more accurate and efficient over time.
